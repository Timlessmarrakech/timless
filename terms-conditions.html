Terms of Service

Last updated: June 20, 2025

1. Booking & Payment

  • A deposit of 30% is required to confirm your booking.
  • Full payment is due 30 days before tour departure.
  • All prices are in USD unless otherwise stated.
  • Payment methods accepted: Credit card, bank transfer.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • 60+ days before departure: Full refund minus deposit.
  • 30-59 days before departure: 50% refund of the total booking cost.
  • 15-29 days before departure: 25% refund of the total booking cost.
  • Less than 15 days before departure: No refund.

We reserve the right to modify tour itineraries due to:

  • Weather conditions

  • Safety concerns
  • Circumstances beyond our control (Force Majeure)

  • Trip cancellation and interruption

  • Lost or stolen personal items during the tour.

6. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate personal and contact information during booking.
