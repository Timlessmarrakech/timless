Ouzoud Waterfalls Day Trip: Morocco's Natural Paradise

1 Day Marrakech, Ouzoud Waterfalls, Middle Atlas Small Group & Private Options 4.8 (65 reviews)

Tour Overview

Embark on a captivating day trip with AMDK TOURS to the spectacular Ouzoud Waterfalls, one of Morocco's most breathtaking natural wonders. Located in the Middle Atlas Mountains, these impressive falls cascade over 110 meters through a lush, verdant valley. This excursion offers a refreshing escape into nature, where you can witness the raw power of the falls, enjoy scenic walks, and encounter the playful Barbary macaques that inhabit the area. It is a day of awe-inspiring sights and unforgettable memories.

Tour Highlights

  • Scenic drive from Marrakech through diverse Moroccan landscapes to the Middle Atlas Mountains.
  • Marvel at the stunning Ouzoud Waterfalls, North Africa's highest cascades.
  • Enjoy guided walks along pathways offering various viewpoints of the multi-tiered falls.
  • Spot and photograph the native Barbary macaques (apes) in their natural habitat.
  • Optional: Take a short boat ride to get closer to the base of the falls and feel the mist.
  • Relax and have lunch at a local restaurant with panoramic views of the waterfalls.

Tour Itinerary

Morning (Approx. 8:00 AM): Departure from Marrakech & Scenic Drive

Your day trip begins with an early morning pickup from your hotel or riad in Marrakech. Settle into our comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle for a scenic drive of approximately 2.5 to 3 hours (around 150-170 km) towards the Ouzoud Waterfalls. The journey takes you northeast of Marrakech, through plains dotted with olive groves and rolling hills, offering glimpses of rural Moroccan life as we approach the foothills of the Middle Atlas Mountains.

Late Morning (Approx. 11:00 AM): Arrival & Exploration of Ouzoud Waterfalls

Upon arrival at Ouzoud, you'll immediately be struck by the impressive sight and sound of the cascading water. Accompanied by a local guide (optional, or included depending on your booking), you'll begin your exploration. We'll take a leisurely walk along well-maintained paths that wind down through olive groves, offering various breathtaking perspectives of the falls. Keep an eye out for the resident Barbary macaques; these playful monkeys are often seen in the trees or near the pathways and are a delight to observe (please remember not to feed them to keep them wild).

Midday: Boat Ride (Optional) & Lunch with a View

As we reach the bottom of the falls, you'll have the option to take a short, charming boat ride that takes you close to the powerful rush of water, allowing you to feel the refreshing mist – a fantastic photo opportunity! Afterwards, we'll settle down for lunch at one of the local restaurants. Many of these establishments offer terraces with spectacular, direct views of the waterfalls, allowing you to enjoy a delicious traditional Moroccan tagine or grilled dish while soaking in the magnificent scenery.

Afternoon: Further Discovery & Relaxation

After lunch, you'll have more free time to explore the area at your own pace. You might choose to walk up the series of steps on the other side of the falls, discovering more viewpoints and perhaps some of the traditional watermills that are still used by locals for grinding grain – the name "Ouzoud" itself means "the act of grinding grain" in Berber. Alternatively, simply relax by the pools, enjoy the natural ambiance, or browse the small souvenir shops for local crafts.

Late Afternoon (Approx. 4:00 PM): Departure & Return to Marrakech

As the afternoon draws to a close, we will regroup and begin our journey back to Marrakech. Reflect on the day's adventure and the stunning natural beauty you've witnessed. You can expect to arrive back at your accommodation in Marrakech in the early evening (around 6:30 PM - 7:00 PM), filled with lasting memories of Morocco's natural paradise.
