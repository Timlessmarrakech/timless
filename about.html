Our Story

Creating Authentic Moroccan Experiences Since 2010

AMDK TOURS was born from a deep-seated passion for sharing the authentic soul of Morocco with the world. Founded by Adil Kharbouch, a native with an intimate connection to the country's diverse landscapes and rich culture, we have grown from a focused, personal operation into a premier tour provider, all while holding true to our core values of authenticity and impeccable service.

Our mission is to design profound, immersive journeys that connect discerning travelers with the real Morocco, far from the well-trodden tourist paths. We are committed to responsible tourism that uplifts local communities and creates unforgettable, lasting memories for our guests.

Adil Kharbouch

Growth & Recognition
