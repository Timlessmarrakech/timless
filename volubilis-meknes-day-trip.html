Volubilis & Meknes Day Trip from Fes: Roman Ruins & Imperial City

1 Day Fes, Volubilis, Moulay Idriss, Meknes Small Group & Private Options 4.7 (78 reviews)

Tour Overview

Embark on a fascinating historical journey from Fes with AMDK TOURS to explore Morocco's most significant ancient and imperial sites. This full-day trip takes you to the magnificent Roman ruins of Volubilis, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and the nearby holy town of Moulay Idriss. Conclude your day in Meknes, one of Morocco's four Imperial Cities, renowned for its grand architecture. This tour offers a rich tapestry of history, culture, and stunning Moroccan landscapes.

Tour Highlights

  • Guided tour of the ancient Roman city of Volubilis (UNESCO World Heritage site).
  • Visit the sacred town of Moulay Idriss Zerhoun for panoramic views and cultural insights.
  • Explore the Imperial City of Meknes (UNESCO World Heritage site), including the monumental Bab Mansour gate.
  • Discover Heri es-Souani (Royal Stables and Granaries) and the Mausoleum of Moulay Ismail.
  • Stroll through the vibrant Place El Hedim in Meknes.
  • Learn about Morocco's rich Roman, Islamic, and Imperial history from expert guides.

Tour Itinerary

Morning (Approx. 9:00 AM): Fes to Volubilis

Your day trip begins with a pickup from your hotel or riad in Fes. We'll embark on a scenic drive of approximately 1.5 hours through the fertile Saiss plains, dotted with olive groves and wheat fields, towards the ancient Roman city of Volubilis. Upon arrival, you'll meet your local guide for an immersive tour of this impressive archaeological site. Wander through the remarkably well-preserved ruins, admiring intricate mosaics, the grand Triumphal Arch, the Basilica, the Capitol, and former Roman villas, learning about the city's history as a thriving outpost of the Roman Empire.

Late Morning: Moulay Idriss Zerhoun

After exploring Volubilis, we take a short drive to the nearby holy town of Moulay Idriss Zerhoun. Perched dramatically on two hills, this town is one of Morocco's most important pilgrimage sites, as it holds the tomb of Moulay Idriss I, the founder of Morocco's first Islamic dynasty. While non-Muslims cannot enter the mausoleum itself, you can explore the charming narrow streets of the town, soak in its spiritual atmosphere, and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and Volubilis from a viewpoint.

Midday - Afternoon: Meknes Exploration

Next, we continue to the Imperial City of Meknes, founded in the 11th century and later made a capital by Sultan Moulay Ismail in the 17th century. We'll stop for lunch at a local restaurant in Meknes (not included). Afterwards, with a local guide, you'll discover the city's historical highlights, including the magnificent Bab Mansour Laleuj, one of the most beautiful gates in Morocco. We'll also visit Heri es-Souani, the vast royal granaries and stables, and the impressive Mausoleum of Moulay Ismail. A stroll through Place El Hedim, the bustling main square opposite Bab Mansour, offers a glimpse into local life.

Late Afternoon: Free Time & Return to Fes (Approx. 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM)

Depending on time, you might have some brief free time to explore the souks of Meknes before we begin our return journey to Fes. You'll be dropped off at your accommodation in the early evening, enriched by a day of historical discovery and cultural immersion.
